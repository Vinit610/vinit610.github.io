  📖  STRATEGY LEGEND
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Every strategy invests ₹10,000 per month (₹20k for SIP+Dip Boost).
  All prices use month-end NIFTY50 closing values from Yahoo Finance.

  Pure SIP
    → Invest ₹10,000 every single month, no conditions, no timing.
       Units bought = ₹10,000 ÷ that month's closing price.

  Dip -1% (MoM)
    → Hold ₹10,000 cash each month. Deploy ALL accumulated cash only
       when NIFTY closes lower than last month by ≥1%.
       A -1% drop is common (~50% of months) so this fires frequently
       and behaves almost like SIP — useful as a near-SIP baseline.

  Dip -3% (MoM)
    → Same, but wait for a ≥3% month-on-month fall.
       A -3% monthly drop is moderate — happens ~25% of months.
       Cash can sit idle for 2-4 months in a bull run.

  Dip -5% (MoM)
    → Wait for a ≥5% single-month fall before deploying.
       These are meaningful corrections — happens ~12% of months.
       E.g. Mar 2020 (-23%), Oct 2018 (-5%), Aug 2015 (-7%).

  Dip -8% (MoM)
    → Only deploy on a severe ≥8% monthly crash.
       Very rare — roughly 5-6 times per decade.
       Cash can sit idle for 6-12+ consecutive months.
       Maximum idle drag, but buys at genuine panic prices.

  SIP + Dip Boost (₹20k/mo total)
    �� Invest ₹10,000/month via SIP regardless (never stops).
       ALSO save a separate ₹10,000/month as a dip reserve.
       Deploy the entire reserve when a ≥3% dip occurs.
       Total capital = ₹20,000/month — NOT directly comparable
       to the ₹10k strategies above, shown for reference only.

  Columns explained:
    Invested  → Total cash put in over the window (₹10k × months)
    Final     → Portfolio value on the last day of the window
    Abs Ret%  → (Final - Invested) / Invested × 100
    XIRR%     → Annualised return accounting for timing of each
                 monthly cashflow (more accurate than simple return)
    Idle%     → % of months cash sat uninvested (dip strategies only)
                 Higher idle% = more cash drag = harder to beat SIP
